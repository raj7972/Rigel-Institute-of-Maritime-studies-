Fast & Reliable Processing

As a recognised leader in seafarer documentation, the Rigel Institute offers efficient, transparent processing of the Liberia Seaman Book — essential for working aboard Panamanian-flagged vessels worldwide.

  • Simple and guided application process with expert support
  • Fast-track approval with quick turnaround time
  • 100% transparent and hassle-free documentation
  • Accepted on all Liberia-flagged vessels worldwide
  • Complete assistance for freshers, ratings, and officers
Navigation

Deck Officers

For navigation officers working on Liberia-flagged vessels, the Liberian Seaman Book (SIRB) is mandatory for legal employment at sea. It ensures full compliance with international maritime regulations and opens access to global career opportunities.

Requirements
  • Valid Certificate of Competency (STCW)
  • Security Training (STCW / IMO requirements)
  • Valid Medical Fitness Certificate (STCW / MLC compliant)
  • Passport copy & passport-size photograph
  • Employment contract / company offer letter
Cruise

Cruise Ship Staff

Complete documentation support for cruise ship staff applying for the Liberian Seaman Book (SIRB). Ensures full compliance with MLC regulations and a smooth international joining process.

Requirements
  • Basic Safety Training (STCW VI/1)
  • Valid Medical Fitness Certificate
  • Passport & passport-size photographs
  • Cruise line employment contract
Engineering

Engine Crew

Complete documentation support for marine engineers and engine crew applying for the Liberian Seaman Book (SIRB). Ensures full compliance with international maritime regulations and smooth placement on Liberia-flagged vessels worldwide.

Requirements
  • Certificate of Competency (Engineering)
  • STCW Basic Safety Training
  • Valid Medical Fitness Certificate
  • Passport & seafarer documents
Offshore

Offshore Vessels

Fast and reliable documentation support for offshore personnel applying for the Liberian Seaman Book (SIRB). Designed for crew working in offshore energy, oil & gas, and support vessel operations on Liberia-flagged vessels worldwide.

Requirements
  • Valid Medical Certificate (STCW / MLC / OPITO approved)
  • STCW Basic Safety Training or BOSIET / FOET (as applicable)
  • Security Duties or Security Awareness Certificate
  • Passport copy & passport-size photograph
  • Employment / hiring contract (if available)
